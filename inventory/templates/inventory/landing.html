About Avis AI

Avis AI is designed to tackle the common corporate challenges of data fragmentation and high latency in operational workflows. Often, retail managers must manually coordinate stock balances across disconnected supply systems, monitor external logistical disruption points, and perform audits.

By binding the python-based Django architecture with the Katana AI cognitive engine, Avis AI streamlines retail operations. It combines live forecasting models, logistics delay warnings, and a block-linked cryptographic auditing system, enabling managers and vendors to cooperate seamlessly inside a unified dashboard.

How It Works

1. Request Order

Store Manager creates a purchase order and sets expected quantities.

2. Check Radar

Logistics Radar scans supplier's city weather to flag delay warning alerts.

3. Vendor Delivers

Supplier logs fulfillment progress and marks order status as Delivered.

4. Audit Sealed

The system cryptographically seals order records into the SHA-256 ledger.

Core Subsystem Features

Katana AI Forecasting

Uses scikit-learn Linear Regression models to evaluate sales history on Pandas dataframes, predicting seasonal demand trends and automatically adjusting optimal safety stock thresholds. Runs asynchronously in background threads.

Logistics Delay Radar

Connects to the OpenWeatherMap API to fetch current atmospheric states in supplier operating cities. Flags active orders automatically with heavy rain, snow, or storm warnings on the manager's panel.

Cryptographic Audits

Constructs a SHA-256 block-linked hash chain for every finalized transaction. If any details are altered directly in the database, the integrity validator flags a mismatched signature immediately.
