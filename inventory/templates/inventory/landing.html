Managing manual purchase orders with multiple suppliers is slow and prone to data entry errors.

Automated Restocking Pipeline Flow

Follow the real-time AI replenishment logistics workflow step-by-step horizontally.

01
PostgreSQL Data Pull

Background Python scripts extract historical sales logs directly from the database.

02
Regression Forecasting

Pandas and Scikit-Learn linear regression models calculate monthly sales velocity.

03
Smart ROP Calculation

The reorder target threshold adjusts dynamically (predicted volume + 20% safety margin buffer).

04
Auto-PO Placement

If active stock falls beneath target levels, a replenishment order draft is generated.

05
Ledger Cryptography

The order details are sealed in the block ledger utilizing mathematical SHA-256 links.

06
Supplier Portal Update

The order draft populates the supplier panel for shipping actions, weather warnings, and deliveries.
